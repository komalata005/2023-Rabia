Skin Biopsy

A diagnostic procedure where a small sample of skin is collected for detailed analysis, aiding in the accurate diagnosis of various skin conditions.

Adult Dermatology

Specialized care tailored to address a wide range of skin concerns specific to adults, from acne and skin aging to skin cancer screenings and treatment.

Paediatric Dermatology

Expert care focused on diagnosing and treating skin conditions in infants, children, and adolescents, ensuring their skin health and overall well-being.

Hydrafacial

A non-invasive skincare treatment that combines cleansing, exfoliation, extraction, hydration, and antioxidant infusion to rejuvenate and nourish the skin, leaving it radiant and refreshed.

Botox & Fillers

Minimally invasive cosmetic treatments involving Botox injections to reduce wrinkles and fillers to restore volume, achieving a smoother, more youthful appearance.

Acne Scar Treatment

Tailored procedures and therapies aimed at minimizing the appearance of acne scars, helping to restore smoother and more even skin texture.

Laser Hair Removal

A safe and effective procedure that uses targeted laser energy to permanently reduce unwanted hair, providing long-lasting smoothness and convenience.

Wart Removals

Professional procedures to safely and effectively eliminate warts, addressing a variety of types and sizes to promote clear and healthy skin.

HIFU

HIFU (High-Intensity Focused Ultrasound): An advanced non-surgical treatment that uses focused ultrasound energy to stimulate collagen production and tighten skin, resulting in a lifted and rejuvenated appearance.
